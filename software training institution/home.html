ONLINE COURSE

Online courses are educational programs delivered over the internet, allowing learners to study anytime and anywhere. These courses cover a wide range of subjects and skills, often including video lectures, interactive exercises, quizzes, and downloadable resources. Designed for flexibility, they cater to various learning paces and needs, making them ideal for students, professionals, or hobbyists seeking to enhance their knowledge or skills. Many online courses are self-paced, while others follow a fixed schedule with instructor-led sessions.




OFFLINE COURSE

An offline course is a traditional, in-person learning program conducted in physical classrooms or training centers. It involves face-to-face interaction with instructors and peers, providing hands-on learning experiences, direct feedback, and personalized guidance. Offline courses are ideal for individuals seeking structured environments and practical demonstrations.




INTERNSHIP

An internship is a short-term work experience offered by organizations to help students or recent graduates gain practical knowledge and skills in a specific field. It allows individuals to apply theoretical learning in a professional environment, build industry connections, and enhance their resumes. Internships may be paid or unpaid and can be part-time or full-time, depending on the organization and role.
